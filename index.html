About

I am a PhD candidate in the Brown University Department of Computer Science and Center for Computational Molecular Biology under the supervision of Dr. Ben Raphael.

As a computer scientist and computational biologist, I develop algorithms and mathematical models to study biological processes. Currently, I work in the field of cancer genomics, comparing the differences between the genomes of healthy tissue and cancerous tissue in the same individual.

During my time as an undergraduate at Tufts University, I worked under the supervision of Drs. Ben Hescott and Lenore Cowen identifying and evaluating fault-tolerant genetic pathways in yeast.
